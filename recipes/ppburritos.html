Quick and Easy Pulled Pork Burritos

Description

A tasty way to use up your leftovers from a pulled pork! Goes well with a little coleslaw.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce can baked beans
  • 8 (10 inch) soft flour tortills
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 pound pulled pork, heated
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 (16 ounce) can mild enchilada sauce

Steps

  1. Pour Beans into a pot and bring to a simmer over medium-low heat.
  2. Place a tortilla on a microwave-safe plate, sprinkle with onion, 2 tablespoons beans, and 2-3 tablespoons pulled pork. Sprinkle Monterey Jack cheese, and roll the tortilla closed. Drizzle over 2 tablespoons of enchilada sauce, and sprinkle with more cheese.
  3. Microwave until heated through and cheese is melted, about 1 minute. Repeat with remaining tortillas and filling ingredients.
